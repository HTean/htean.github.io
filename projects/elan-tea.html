Elan Tea — Online Retail Store

A brand-first e-commerce experience for a specialty tea company — built to make product discovery feel elegant and purchasing feel effortless.

The Brief

Elan Tea needed more than a storefront — they needed a brand platform. The challenge was balancing visual storytelling (artisan products, origin stories, ceremony) with a conversion-optimized shopping experience. Too much editorial and you lose buyers; too transactional and you lose the brand.

What I Built

A full e-commerce site featuring a brand-focused landing page, product catalogue with rich detail pages, and a streamlined checkout flow powered by Stripe. Product data is managed through a lightweight SQL backend so the client can update inventory without touching code.

Technical Approach

The stack prioritizes reliability and simplicity over complexity. Node.js handles the backend API and order management; Stripe processes payments securely without storing card data server-side. The frontend is server-rendered for fast initial load and good SEO, critical for an e-commerce brand building organic traffic.

Reflections

The most interesting design challenge was the product page — making a simple item like loose-leaf tea feel worth $30 through photography, origin narrative, and brewing guides. Conversion follows when the product feels worth it, not just when the checkout is fast.
